# toggle (turn on or off) an asset's state:
# relies on on+off.txt!

On "toggle ASSET":
	the ASSET is on;
	turn the ASSET off;
	reply "ok, the ASSET is now off";
	if not, turn the ASSET on;
	then, reply "ok, the ASSET is now on".
#On "help", reply "to toggle the ASSET".
On "toggle the ASSET", toggle ASSET.
